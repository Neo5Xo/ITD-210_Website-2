Our Story

“I think dogs are the most amazing creatures: they give unconditional love. For me they are the role model for being alive."

We are located in rural Virginia Beach. We live on over 6 1/2 acres of land so our dogs and puppies get plenty of fresh air and exercise. We all enjoy lots of nature and many beautiful sunsets.​

​ Over 25 years we have occasionally bred our own personal dogs including; Bouviers, Corgis, Dobermans, and Golden Retrievers. After raising our two boys with Dobermans, we discovered Standard Poodles. We loved their intelligence, agility, and non-shedding. After some diligent research, we discovered GOLDENDOODLES.

​ We began breeding Goldendoodles in 2003. We kept a Goldendoodle from our first breeding. She is the famous….Shenleigh O’Doodle! She has become an ambassador for the breed! We have established ourselves a top reputation for producing smart, happy, healthy, well-structured dogs with great temperaments and intelligence. We earned the distinction of being listed as recommended breeders at the worldwide website Goldendoodles.com.
